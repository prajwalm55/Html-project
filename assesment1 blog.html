Blog on Child Labour

Published on: July 15, 2024

Child labour remains a poignant issue that demands global attention. Despite concerted efforts towards eradication, millions of children worldwide are still deprived of their fundamental rights and forced into exploitative labour practices.

This blog sheds light on the critical aspects of child labour, its implications, and the ongoing efforts to combat this social menace.
